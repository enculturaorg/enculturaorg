# Encultura

Encultura is a protocol for the development of human navigational capacity.

Its purpose is to cultivate the conditions under which individuals, communities, institutions, and intelligent systems can participate in healthy developmental processes through verifiable governance, protocol-based coordination, and stewardship of network health.

Encultura views human development as a navigational challenge rather than a classificatory one. Its focus is the cultivation of navigational capacity across multiple worldspaces while preserving the integrity of the commons.
