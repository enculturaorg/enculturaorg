# Encultura Membership

## Purpose

Membership exists to support the stewardship and development of the Encultura Protocol.

Membership is not primarily a status designation. It is a commitment to participation in the developmental, governance, and stewardship responsibilities of the protocol.

---

## Membership Classes

### Founding Member

A participant recognized as contributing to the establishment of the protocol during its Genesis Phase.

### Funding Member

A participant who materially supports the continued development and operation of Encultura.

Funding support may include financial contributions, infrastructure support, technical development, governance work, educational contributions, or other forms of recognized stewardship.

### Member

A participant formally recognized by protocol governance.

### Anjuman Member

A participant entrusted with governance responsibilities under the protocol.

---

## Genesis Membership

During the Genesis Phase:

did:web:encultura.org is recognized as:

* Founding Steward
* Founding Member
* Funding Member
* Initial Governance Authority

until governance is distributed through the formation of the Anjuman.

---

## Membership Verification

Membership claims are valid only when documented through published protocol records or signed attestations.
